Getting Help

For questions, bug reports, or feedback about the WhatTheBug app, please contact us:

support@whatthebug.app

Common Topics

  • Identification accuracy: Results are generated by AI and may occasionally be incorrect. Use them as a guide.
  • In-app purchases: Purchases and subscriptions are managed by Apple. For billing or refund issues, contact Apple Support.
  • Camera issues: Ensure the app has camera permission in Settings → Privacy → Camera.
  • App crashes or bugs: Describe what happened and when. Include your device model and iOS version if possible.
